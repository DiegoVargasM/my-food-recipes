Here you will learn how to make the perfect Peruvian Ceviche

Recipe description

White ceviche is one of the most common ceviches, it is usually made with white fish, but it can vary depending on the region where it is prepared.

Despite its name, the ingredients make this dish colorful. It is delicious and very healthy, since it allows you to take full advantage of the nutrients in fish, such as omega-3, phosphorus and vitamin D, among others.

It is an excellent dish for those who follow a diet and for people with hypertension problems.

Ingredients

  • 1kg. White fish fillet
  • ¼ Cup coriander
  • 2 Limo peppers or serrano peppers
  • 6 Lemons or limes
  • 3 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 Purple onions
  • ½ Red bell pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. To begin with, we chop the fish fillets into small squares or cubes of approximately 1 centimeter; then chop the onions into fine julienne strips and the chili peppers into small pieces.
  2. In a deep container, preferably glass or metal, add the fish, the peppers and the onions, then squeeze the lemon juice over them, season with salt and pepper to taste and leave to marinate for about 10 minutes in the fridge so that the fish cooks with lemon juice.
  3. Meanwhile, chop the pepper into small pieces and chop the coriander leaves into small pieces.
  4. Remove the fish from the fridge and add the cilantro and the pepper; drain a little of the lemon juice and add the tablespoons of olive oil.
  5. Mix all the ingredients and taste the taste, if necessary add a little more salt and pepper and then mix again.
  6. You can serve immediately, but if you want, you can leave another 5 minutes marinating in the fridge to intensify the flavor a little more.
